BARANGAY CERTIFICATE

A Barangay Certificate is an official document issued by the barangay that certifies certain information about an individual, typically a resident of that specific barangay. This document may serve various purposes, such as verifying residency, affirming good moral character, or providing clearance for specific transactions or activities. The certificate usually includes basic details such as the individual's name, address, and other pertinent information relevant to the purpose for which the certificate is requested.


  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure


Application

Eligibility

Residents of a specific barangay.

Where to process
  • Barangay Hall in your resident barangay.

Requirements
  • Valid ID
  • Any document proving your residency
  • Note:
    Different barangays may have unique requirements and procedures. While the provided information reflects common practices, it is advisable to contact your local barangay office for any additional or specific details related to obtaining a Barangay Certificate in your area.
Procedure
  1. Visit the barangay hall.
  2. Approach a staff member and express your intention to obtain a barangay certification.
  3. Expect to be asked about the purpose of needing the certification.
  4. Once your request is approved, patiently wait for the processing of your barangay certificate.
  5. Finally, receive the issued Barangay Certificate.
