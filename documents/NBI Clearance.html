NBI CLEARANCE

It is a government-issued document that certifies an individual with no pending criminal case or record used for visa applications, job applications, and other official purposes. There is no minimum age requirement for this type of document.


  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure
  • Fees


Application

Eligibility

Individuals who are 18 years old and above.

Minors(individuals below 18 years old) may also obtain an NBI clearance in certain special cases, such as adoption or travel abroad, where the clearance is required by specific agencies

Where to process
  • Davao City National Bureau of Investigation XI - SEMRO
    • 3JP6+F7Q, J.P. Laurel Ave,
    Poblacion District, Davao City,
    Davao del Sur

Requirements
  • At least two (2) valid IDs, you may choose from the following:
    • Philippine Passport
    • Voter's ID
    • Driver’s License
    • Professional Regulation Commission(PRC) License
    • SSS(Social Security System) ID
    • GSIS UMID
    • Postal ID
    • School ID
    • TIN ID
    • PhilHealth ID
    • Alien Certificate of Registration
    • Senior Citizen ID
  • NBI Appointment
Procedure
  1. Visit https://clearance.nbi.gov.ph and register for an account online
  2. Supply the necessary information.
  3. Sign in and schedule an appointment online.
  4. Fill out the necessary details.
  5. Choose your preferred NBI Branch, schedule, and clearance fee.
  6. Complete your payment then use the NBI Clearance Online Payment Reference Number for your online transaction.
  7. Your appointment will then reflect on the website. On the day of your appointment, bring at least two (2) valid IDs and bring your NBI Clearance Reference Number.
  8. Then, a fingerprint biometrics and photo capture is needed to verify your identity.
  9. Finally, you may claim your NBI clearance on the releasing section.

Fees
  • NBI Clearance Fee - Php 130.00
  • Service Fee - Php 25.00
  • Note:
    This is free for first- time job seekers in compliance with Republic Act No. 11261
    • You could avail of this privilege once and for once copy of clearance only
    • You need to present a duly issued Barangay Certificate from place of residence stating that you are a resident for at least six (6) months and a first time jobseeker
    • The issued Barangay Certificate can be used within one year from the date of issuance.
