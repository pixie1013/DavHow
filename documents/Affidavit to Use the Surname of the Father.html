REGISTRATION OF LEGAL INSTRUMENTS FOR R.A. 9255 AFFIDAVIT TO USE THE SURNAME OF THE FATHER (AUSF)

A registrable document that allows an illegitimate child to use the surname of the father.


  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure
  • Fees


Eligibility

All registered illegitimate person born in Davao City.

Where to process
  • Civil Registration Office-Legal Instrument and Court Degrees Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Ground Floor, SP Building, San Pedro Street, Davao City
    (082) 241-1000 local 364/365
    lcr@davaocity.gov.ph
    ATTY. LEO ANTHONY R. BRACEROS


Requirements
  • Certificate of live birth of the child – 1 PSA copy and 1 certified true copy
  • Valid identification cards of both parents – 1 photocopy
  • Personal appearance of both parents
  • Personal appearance of the child (if needed)
Procedure
  • For previously registered birth:
    1. Present/submit Civil Registry records together with supporting documents as required for evaluation.
    2. Fill-up application form
    3. Sign the prepared affidavit and have it notarized to a lawyer and pay the required fees at City Treasurer’s Office
    4. Submit filled-up application forms with Official Receipt (O.R.) to administrative division for recording.
    5. Receive the registered legal instrument and form 1-A with annotation

  • For not yet registered birth:
    • Present the prepared form 102 from clinics and hospital with AUSF



Fees
  • If father is a Filipino - Php 300.00
  • If father is a foreigner - Php 700.00
  • Certification Fee (for previously registered birth) - Php 100.00
  • Admission of Paternity Fee (if applicable) - Php 300.00
