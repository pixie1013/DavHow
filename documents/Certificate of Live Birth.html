CERTIFICATE OF LIVE BIRTH

The Certificate of Live Birth (COLB) is a legal document created shortly after a child is born. This is created to efficiently record all the important details of their birth including not only the date, time, and place of birth but also essential information about the child's parents, providing a clear and legally recognized account of the family background.

Timely Registration of Birth

Eligibility

Persons born in Davao City from 0 to 30 Days old (within one (1) month from birth).

Where to process
  • City Civil Registrar’s Office - Birth Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Ground Floor, SP Building, San Pedro Street, Davao City
    (082) 241-1000 local 364/365
    lcr@davaocity.gov.ph
    ATTY. LEO ANTHONY R. BRACEROS
  • District Office Civil Registry
    • 1. District heads goes to the main office twice a week or as the need arises.

    2. Requirements and payments at the district office are the same with the main office.

Requirements
  • For hospital born:
    • Filled-up Certificate of Live Birth Form (COLB) – 4 original
    • Marriage Contract (if parents are married) (1 original copy and 1 photocopy

  • For home born:
    • Barangay Certification (1 original copy)
    • Certificate of Live Birth Form signed by the Hilot/ Traditional Midwife – 4 original
    • Marriage Contract – 1 original copy, 1 photocopy)
Procedure
  • For hospital/clinic born:
    • Legitimate
      1. Go to Window 19 and submit Certificate of Live Birth (COLB) form to the Receiving Clerk for evaluation.
      2. Receive registered copy of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth)

    • Illegitimate (Applicable for hospital and home born)
      1. Go to Window 19 and submit prepared Certificate of Live Birth (COLB) form to the Receiving Clerk
      2. Present Valid ID of the mother and father.
      3. The mother will sign the AUSF (Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father) and have it notarized by a lawyer
      4. Proceed to Window 7 for registration of the AUSF (Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father)
      5. Go back to Window 17 for the registration of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth)
      6. Receive registered copy of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth)


  • For Home Born (Applicable for Legitimate and illegitimate child)
    1. Submit requirements to Receiving Clerk (Window 17) and wait for processing
    2. Receive registered copy of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth)



Delayed Registration of Birth

Eligibility

All persons born in Davao City after 1 month from the day the child was born.

Where to process
  • City Civil Registrar’s Office - Birth Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Ground Floor, SP Building, San Pedro Street, Davao City
    (082) 241-1000 local 364/365
    lcr@davaocity.gov.ph
    ATTY. LEO ANTHONY R. BRACEROS
  • District Office Civil Registry
    • 1. District heads goes to the main office twice a week or as the need arises.

    2. Requirements and payments at the district office are the same with the main office.

Requirements

At least 3 or 4 of the following:

  • Negative Result (for 2 years old and above)
  • Affidavit of two (2) disinterested person who might have witnessed or known the birth of the child (for 1 year old and above) – 1 original
  • Any 2 of the following documentary evidences which shows the name of the child, place of birth and the name of parents:
    • Baptismal/Dedication Certificate – 1 original, 1 photocopy
    • School Records (Form 137-E or Transcript) – 1 original, 1 photocopy
    • Voter’s Certificate – 1 original, 1 photocopy
    • Philhealth Members Data Record – 1 original
    • Passport/ Postal ID Card – 1 original, 1 photocopy
    • Service Record (If employed) – 1 original
    • Social Security System (SSS) Record (E/E-4/RS-1) – 1 original, 1 photocopy
    • Others:
      • Immunization Card – 1 original, 1 photocopy
      • Barangay Certificate (for 12 years old and below for home deliveries) – 1 original, 1 photocopy
      • Old cedula – 1 original, 1 photocopy
  • Marriage Contract (Parents & Applicant if married) - 1 original, 1 photocopy; or
  • Birth certificate of siblings or birth certificate of son or daughter (eldest) – 1 original, 1 photocopy
  • Community Tax Certificate (cedula) – 1 original

Note: In claiming a birth certificate, the client must present the following:

  • Valid ID
  • If through a representative, authorization letter or Special Power of Attorney (SPA) with a valid ID of the authorizing person.
Procedure
  1. Fill-out slip at the Front Desk and submit to Window 16 for verification of record

  2. Submit request slip and required supporting documents for registration at Window 19

  3. Go to Window 19 and submit approved documents for typing of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth) Form

  4. For illegitimate child, it is advised to register Affidavit to Use Surname of Father at window 17 and to go back to window 19

  5. Get claim stub and come back after 10 day- posting

  6. Receive registered copy of COLB (Certificate of Live Birth)



Out-of-Town Registration of Birth

Eligibility

All persons residing in Davao but born outside Davao City.

Where to process
  • City Civil Registrar’s Office - Birth Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Ground Floor, SP Building, San Pedro Street, Davao City
    (082) 241-1000 local 364/365
    lcr@davaocity.gov.ph
    ATTY. LEO ANTHONY R. BRACEROS
  • District Office Civil Registry
    • 1. District heads goes to the main office twice a week or as the need arises.

    2. Requirements and payments at the district office are the same with the main office.

Requirements

At least 3 or 4 of the following:

  • Affidavit of Registration to be corroborated by 2 witnesses – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Negative result from (PSA) Philippine Statistics Authority – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Baptismal certificate (certified) – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • School records (form 137-E or transcript) – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Voter’s Affidavit for 18 years old and above – 1 original copy, 3 photocopies
  • Marriage Contract of Parents – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Marriage Contract of applicant (if female & married) – 1 original copy, 3 photocopies
  • Community Tax Certificate (new) – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • E1 form (SSS) Social Security System (microfilmed) – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • NBI (National Bureau of Investigation) clearance (old) / passport – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Service record (if employed – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Valid ID card – 1 original, 3 photocopies
  • Postal Money Order/Peso Pack payable to the Municipality/City Civil – 1 original, 3 photocopies
Procedure
  1. Submit required supporting documents in Window 19.
  2. Fill-up draft and sign Certificate of Live Birth (COLB).
  3. Pay at the cashier in Window 15 (Fee- Php 100.00).
  4. Get the claim stub in Window 19.
  5. Come back after 5 working days (Window 19), get the prepared COLB (Certificate of Live Birth) for mailing at LBC Courier/Peso Pack and give back a photocopy of the receipt/proof of mailing.
  6. Receive a complete set of documents for mailing.
  7. Submit the photocopy of the courier receipt and the claim stub.
