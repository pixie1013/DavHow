OCCUPATIONAL PERMIT

Occupational Permit is issued to any worker or employee who is not a holder of a validly issued Professional Tax Receipts, whether working on temporary and permanent basis. Occupational Permit is renewed annually on or before January 31. Late renewal is imposed a penalty of 25%

  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure
  • Fees


Application

Eligibility

Any employee or worker who is a Filipino citizen and 18 years of age at the time of application. Foreigner may apply if he has already secured an Alien Employment Permit from the Department of Labor and Employment. In instances where the law allows a person below 18 years of age to work, the applicant shall submit an Affidavit of Consent from his/her parent or guardian

Where to process
  • City Mayor's Office - Permits & Licenses Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Building City Hall Dr,
    Poblacion District, Davao City,
    Davao del Sur

Requirements
  • Application Form (1 original)
  • Certificate of Employment
  • Affidavit of Consent for minors (below 18 years old)
  • Affidavit of Consent for minors (below 18 years old)
  • Additional Requirements for Entertainers, Masseurs, Bar Attendants:
    1. Birth Certificate (1 original NSO Authenticated)
    2. Affidavit of consent with valid ID of parent or qualified guardian for 18 – 20 years old applicants (1 original)
    3. Health ID (1 original)
Procedure

Walk-In Application

  1. Fill out application form
  2. Submit duly filledout application form and supporting documents.
  3. If below age requirement, Applicant must present himself/herself for an interview
  4. Receive Order of Payment (OP)
  5. Submit Order of Payment and receive Official Receipt
  6. Submit Official Receipt
  7. Claim Occupational Permit
Procedure

Online Application

  1. Application (Payment may Precede Online Application)
  2. Login to https://occupationalpermit.davaocity.gov.ph/ and select Create Application for New or Occupational Permit Renewal for Renewal
  3. Fill out Online Application Form
  4. Upload online the following documents:
    1. Certificate of Employment
    2. Health Certificate (If Job Requires)
    3. Birth Certificate / Affidavit of Consent (if Applicant is MINOR)
    4. Birth Certificate / Affidavit of Consent (if Applicant is MINOR)
  5. If below age requirement, Applicant must present himself/herself for an interview
  6. Submit OP(Order of Payment). and payment (Online Payment available. May precede Application without order of payment especially for online applicants) and Receive Official Receipt
  7. Submit Official Receipt
  8. Proceed to https://occupationalpermit.davaocity.gov.ph/, select Check Application Status, and download the Occupational Permit, or proceed to the link automatically sent by the Online Occupational Permit System though e-mail or SMS and download the Occupational Permit
  9. Claim Occupational Permit
