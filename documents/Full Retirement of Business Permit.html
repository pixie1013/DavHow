FULL PERMIT OF BUSINESS PERMIT

Full retirement of Business Permit is issued to any natural or juridical entity whose business operations have ceased.


  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure
  • Fees


Application

Eligibility

Any natural or juridical entity who is a holder of a valid issued business permit but whose business operations had ceased

Where to process
  • City Mayor's Office - Permits & Licenses Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Building City Hall Dr,
    Poblacion District, Davao City,
    Davao del Sur

Requirements
  • Latest Business Permit (1 original or 1 certified copy)

  • Affidavit of Full Retirement indicating the reason and the effective date of retirement if Single Proprietorship. (1 original copy)

  • Partnership Resolution duly signed by all the Partners stating the reason, effective date of retirement and the authorized representative indicated therein if Partnership. (1 original copy)

  • Board Resolution or Secretaryâ€™s Certificate stating the reason and effective date of retirement, the authorized represented indicated therein if other forms of ownership (1 original copy)

  • Valid I.D. of the Taxpayer if Sole proprietorship (1 photocopy copy)

  • Valid I.D. of all the Partners (1 photocopy each)

  • Valid I.D. of the President and authorized representative for Board Resolution or of the Secretary and the authorized representative for Secretaryâ€™s Certificate if other forms of ownership (1 photocopy each)

  • Special Power of Attorney for Single Proprietorship if represented (1 copy original) and valid I.D. of the representative (1 photocopy)

  • Business Identification Plate

Procedure
  1. Submit requirements to the licensing officers
  2. File with the City Treasurer (Central or District depending on business location) the application for retirement and supporting documents.
  3. Submit recommended approved application for retirement, supporting documents and Business Identification Plate and receive claim stub

Fees
  • Tax Obligation based on gross sales.
  • Late filing fee for application filed more than 30 days from cessation =P1,000 to P5,000
