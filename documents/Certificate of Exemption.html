CERTIFICATE OF EXEMPTION

This legal certificate certifies that an organization or an individual is not liable to pay taxes on certain income from transactions.


  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure
  • Fees


Application

Eligibility

Persons with low income/no income who want to avail of Scholarship and Job/Livelihood Programs.

Where to process
    Revenue District Office (RDO) - Administrative Section (AS)
    • BIR Regional Office Building Bolton Extension, Davao City
    • District Office No. 113A: Bolton Ext., Barangay 1-A Poblacion, Davao City (at the back of the Regional Office Building--right wing)
      • Area of Jurisdiction:
        • Baguio
        • Calinan
        • Marilog
        • Toril
        • Talomo
        • Tugbok
    • District Office No. 113B: Bolton Ext., Davao City (at the back of the BIR Regional Office Building-left wing)
      • Area of Jurisdiction:
        • Agdao
        • Buhangin
        • Bunawan
        • Davao City
        • District A to D
        • Paquibato
        • Poblacion

Requirements
  • Certification of Low Income/No Income signed by the Barangay Chairman of the place where the applicant resides; (1 original)
  • Duly Notarized Affidavit of Low Income/No Income; (1 original)
  • Proof of Payment of â‚± 100.00 Certification Fee and â‚± 30.00 loose Documentary Stamp Tax. (1 photocopy).
  • If transacting through a representative:
    • Special Power of Attorney (SPA) executed by the taxpayer-applicant; (1 original)
    • Any government-issued ID of the taxpayer and authorized representative; (1 photocopy).

    Note: IDs shall be presented and should be readable, untampered and contains consistent information with the documents presented upon application.

Procedure
  1. Submit the complete documentary requirements in the Administrative Section.
  2. Pay Certification Fee and loose Documentary Stamp Tax at the Collection Section and present the proof of payment to the Administrative Section.
  3. Receive requested documents in the Administrative Section.

  • Certification Fee - Php 100.00
  • Documentary Stamp Tax - Php 30.00
