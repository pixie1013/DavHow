CANCELLATION OF BUSINESS PERMIT

Cancellation of Business Permit is offered to any natural or juridical entity whose application for Business Permit is being applied and encoded in the system but the applicant later decided not to push through the said application for any valid reason. Cancellation should be applied within three (3) months from date of application of Business Permit. Cancellation of Business Permit cannot be provided to an applicant who has any record with the City Treasurerâ€™s Office.

  • Eligibility
  • Where to process
  • Requirements
  • Procedure


Application

Eligibility

Any natural or juridical entity that has valid reason to cancel duly processed application for new business permit.

Where to process
  • City Mayor's Office - Permits & Licenses Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Building City Hall Dr,
    Poblacion District, Davao City,
    Davao del Sur

Requirements
  • Application Form (1 original)

  • Affidavit of Cancellation stating reason of cancellation for Sole Proprietorship (2 original copies)

  • I.D. of Taxpayer (1 photocopy)

  • Special Power of Attorney stating the authority and capacity of the authorized representative (1 original and 1 photocopy)

  • Partnership Resolution stating the reason of cancellation and the authority and capacity of the authorized representative for partnership (1 original and 1 photocopy)

  • Board Resolution or Secretaryâ€™s Certificate stating reason of cancellation and the authority and capacity of the authorized representative for other form of ownership (1 original and 1 photocopy)

  • I.D. of Partners (1 photocopy each)

  • I.D. of authorized signatory for other form of ownership (1 photocopy)

  • I.D. of authorized representative (1 photocopy)

Procedure
  1. Client submits complete requirements to EDP Head and receive claim stub.
  2. If approved to be canceled, client claims duly canceled application and other documents
  3. If recommended for retirement, receive application and other documents for reprocessing application under retirement service
