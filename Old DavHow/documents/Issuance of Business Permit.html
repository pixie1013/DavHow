ISSUANCE OF BUSINESS PERMIT

It shall be unlawful for any person or entity to conduct or engage in any business, trade or occupation within the territorial jurisdiction of the City of Davao for which a permit is required for the proper supervision and enforcement of existing laws and ordinances without first having secured a permit therefore from the City Mayor and paying the necessary fees to the City Treasurer.


The Business Permit is granted for a period of not more than one (1) year and shall expire on the thirty-first (31st) of December following the date of issuance unless revoked or surrendered earlier. The Business Permit issued shall be renewed within the month of January. It shall have a continuing validity only upon renewal thereof and payment of the corresponding fee.

Application

Eligibility

Any natural or juridical person who will be conducting and engaging in any business, trade or occupation within the territorial jurisdiction of Davao City

Where to process
  • City Mayor's Office - Permits & Licenses Division
    • 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    Building City Hall Dr,
    Poblacion District, Davao City,
    Davao del Sur

Requirements for Permit of New Business

  • Duly filled-out and notarized Unified Application Form (1 original)
  • Sketch of Business location (1 original copy)
  • Barangay Clearance (1 original copy)
  • Valid ID of applicant and Authorized Signatory (1 photocopy each)
  • Certified List of Employees / Certification of no Employee (1 original and 1 photocopy)
  • Valid DTI Registration (1 photocopy)
  • SEC Registration (1 certified copy), Article of Incorporation and By-laws for Partnership and Corporation (1 photocopy- complete set)
  • Certificate of Cooperation (1 Certified Copy; Article of Cooperation and By-laws (1 photocopy – complete set).
  • DOLE Certificate of Registration (1 Certified Copy), if applicable
  • Special Power of Attorney (1 current and original) for represented Sole Proprietorship
  • Partnership Resolution (1 current and original) for represented Partnership
  • Secretary’s Certificate or Board Resolution for other form of ownership (1 current and original)
  • Valid I.D. of representatives (1 photocopy each)
  • Contract of Lease and copy of the Proof of ownership by the Lessor if details are not reflected on the contract or if Lessor has no Business Permit (1 photocopy)
  • Any proof of ownership if Business location is owned
  • Notarized consent and copy of any proof of ownership if details are not reflected on consent if permitted to use the business location (1 original)
  • OR/CR of vehicle (1 photocopy each vehicle)
  • Notarized Consent for use of the vehicle if not owned by the applicant.
  • Valid Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (1 original and 1 Business Bureau copy)
  • Other Requirements depending on the Business Activity declared
Procedure for Permit of New Business

Walk-in Application

  1. Submit application and requirements to the Licensing Officer
  2. Receive Disapproved Application or System Approval Slip (O.P.)
  3. Issuance of Assessment of Local Tax and Official Receipt
  4. Receive the assessment
  5. Pay tax, fees and charges
  6. Receive Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (FSIC)
  7. Submit the recommended approval of application of new / renewal / amendment of business permit, official receipts and pertinent documents.
  8. Receive Business Permit, Business Identification Plate, Sticker, FSIC and applicant’s copy of the requirements.


Online Application

  1. Client may login to appbts.davaocity.gov.ph
  2. Fill up the online application form completely.
  3. Upload Required Documents
  4. Check the box if OK and then press submit.
  5. Client receives SMS or e-mail notification with access code if application is submitted successfull
  6. Confirm Application
  7. Check the status of the application. If approved by ROs, proceed to payment of fees. If pending, comply with requirements. If denied, proceed to ROs denying the application for further information.
  8. One-time payment of taxes, charges and fees (online or window).
  9. Client downloads and prints the Business Permit. Business plate and sticker may be delivered by Business Bureau employee or claimed at the office.
Requirements for Renewal of Business Permit
  • Previous Mayor’s Permit (1 copy)
  • List of Employees stating the Name and Position of the Employees (1 copy)
  • Valid I.D. of the Taxpayer for Sole Proprietorship, of Partners if Partnership and of Authorized Signatory for other form of ownership (1 photocopy)
  • For Sole Proprietorship, if represented, Special Power of Attorney, indicating specific authority and capacity granted to representative.
  • For Partnership, Partners Resolution, indicating specific authority and capacity granted to representative.
  • For other form of ownership, Board Resolution or Secretary’s Certificate, indicating specific authority and capacity granted to representative.
  • Valid I.D. of representative (1 photocopy)
  • Valid Fire Safely Inspection Certificate (1 photocopy)
  • Specific requirement for specific line of business
Procedure for Renewal of Business Permit

Walk-in Application

  1. Submit application and requirements to the Licensing Officer
  2. Receive Disapproved Application or System Approval Slip (O.P.)
  3. Issuance of Assessment of Local Tax and Official Receipt
  4. Receive the assessment
  5. Pay tax, fees and charges
  6. Receive Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (FSIC)
  7. Submit the recommended approval of application of new of business permit, official receipts and pertinent documents.
  8. Receive Business Permit, Business Identification Plate, Sticker, FSIC and applicant’s copy of the requirements.
  9. Receive Business Permit, Business Identification Plate, Sticker, FSIC and applicant’s copy of the requirements
Procedure for Renewal of Business Permit

Online Application

  1. Client may login to appbts.davaocity.gov.ph and update his/her contact info: email address, contact nos. and upload documents required documents for updating of details:
    1. Attached: IDs (owner and authorized representative-if transacting person is not the owner)
    2. Selfie holding an ID of owner and authorized representative
    3. SPA for single proprietorship and SEC. CERT. for Corporation
  2. Client will receive thru email and SMS an access code (once access code has been received)
  3. Taxpayer may now renew his/her business permit: Applicant to fill up the web forms and submit together with the documents required.
  4. Check the status of the application. If approved by ROs, proceed to payment of fees. If pending, comply with requirements. If denied, proceed to ROs denying the application for further information.
  5. One-time payment of taxes, charges and fees (online or window).
  6. The client can pay online.
  7. Client downloads and prints the Business Permit. Business plate and sticker may be delivered by Business Bureau employee or claimed at the office
