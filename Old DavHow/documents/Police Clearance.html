POLICE CLEARANCE

A police clearance is an official document issued by law enforcement agencies that confirms whether an individual has a criminal record or charges. It provides a summary of an individual's criminal history, if any, and is often required for various purposes, such as employment or immigration. Obtaining a police clearance involves a background check by the police to ensure the person's criminal history is accurately represented.

Application

Eligibility

Individuals who are 18 years old and above.

Where to process
  • Online (National Police Clearance System (NCPS))

Requirements
  • Two Valid Issued ID
    • Armed Forces of the Philippines(AFP) ID
    • Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) ID
    • Barangay Residence Certification
    • GSIS UMID
    • LTO Driver's License
    • Marina ID
    • PAG-IBIG ID
    • PWD ID
    • PhilHealth ID
    • Postal ID
    • School ID with Registration Form
    • TIN ID
    • Alien Certificate of Registration
    • BJMP ID
    • Birth Certificate
    • IBP ID
    • LTOPF ID
    • OFW ID
    • PCG ID
    • PRC License
    • Passport
    • Philippine Identification
    • SSS ID/ SSS UMID
    • Senior Citizen's ID
    • Voter's ID

  • Reference Number
  • Official Receipt of payment
Procedure
  1. Online Registration and Appointment Setting
    • Visit the https://pnpclearance.ph to access the system.
    1. Account Registration
      • For first time applicants, Account Registration is required. Fill up the necessary information in order to create an account. The said information will be used as a reference in the log-in process.
        (Take note that an existing or active personal email account is required. And make sure to remember your account and password.)
      • Log-in to your email address. Check your inbox for Email Confirmation and click the message from NCPS to verify your registered account. (Take note that there are instances that the email confirmation will not appear in your inbox. In cases like this, check the email confirmation on your spam messages.)
      • Click the link to continue. You will then be directed to the website’s log-in page.
    2. Signing-in
      • Sign-in using the email address and password that was used during the registration process.
    3. Clearance Application
      1. Edit your Profile
        • Click on the Edit Profile Button
        • Supply all other needed information required for the transaction
        • Review and check if there are misspelled information
        • Click Save Profile
      2. Set an Appointment
        • Click the Application of Clearance button to start
        • Choose the Police Station where you will process the transaction or get your clearance
        • Select the date, either AM or PM, from the list of available schedules
        • Click Next to proceed.
        • Click Land Bank of the Philippines button and Save Appointment
        • Save or Note the generated Reference Number
        • Click Pay to Land Bank button to proceed to payment
Procedure
  1. Payment
    • You will be directed to the LBP ePayment Portal website for the payment process.
      • Available payment options:
        • Via Landbank ATM
        • Via G-Cash
        • Via BancNet Cards
    • Follow the process needed for LBP payment process, Enter the account number and PIN and wait for the transaction details and payment summary to display.
  2. Photo and Fingerprint Capture
    • Applicants can proceed to the selected Police Station on his/her scheduled date. Present the following requirements to the Police Station.
      • Two (2) valid issued IDs
      • Reference Number
      • Official Receipt of payment
    • Before the release of Clearance, applicant shall be required to have his/her biometrics, signature and photo captured.
  3. Release of Clearance
    • For without “Hit”: After completion of steps 1 to 3, wait for your name to be called for the release of your clearance.
    • For with “Hit”: Wait for the advice or instruction of the Verification Officer for the verification process.
    • Once settled, clearance will be released.
